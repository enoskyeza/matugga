About

Chairperson's Message

We are a comprehensive farmers center, offering a full range of farm inputs and public health products. Licensed by MAAIF and NDA, we supply and distribute an array of animal health, crop health, and public health products. We collaborate with toll manufacturers worldwide and are in the process of establishing our own manufacturing facility in Uganda.

Our product lineup includes pesticides, herbicides, insecticides, rodenticides, fungicides, farm tools, fertilizers, as well as vaccines and disease control products for birds and animals. We also offer animal nutrition and supplements, among other items. We provide a range of farm service extension services, which include consultation, education and training, technical assistance, market information, market research among others.

Mrs. Fiona Migadde

Executive Chair of Board
